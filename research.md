# ValuationGenie — Instant AI Business Valuations for First-Time Buyers

## Executive Summary

**ValuationGenie** provides instant, affordable AI-powered business valuations for first-time buyers and sellers. We democratize access to accurate valuations that traditionally cost $5K-$50K from brokers and appraisal firms.

---

## Market Opportunity

### AI Market Growth
- AI market projected to reach **$1.81 trillion by 2030** (CNBC)
- AI business valuation tools emerging as niche within broader AI fintech

### Small Business Transactions
- **~2,368 small businesses sold in Q1 2025** alone (ASBN)
- Median sale price: **$349,000** (BizBuySell)
- Service sector transactions up 4% year-over-year

### The Pain Point
- Traditional business valuations cost **$8,000-$50,000+** (INNP)
- Brokers often provide free estimates that favor their interests
- First-time buyers have no reliable, unbiased way to assess fair value
- Free online calculators are overly simplistic and inaccurate

## Competitor Landscape

| Competitor | Focus | Weakness |
|------------|-------|----------|
| **BizBuySell** | Marketplace listings | No AI valuation, just comparables |
| **Traditional Appraisers** | $5K+ valuations | Expensive, slow (weeks) |
| **Business Brokers** | Free estimates | Biased toward closing deals |
| **Free Online Calculators** | Basic formulas | Oversimplified, no context |
| **FE International** | SaaS valuations | Enterprise-focused only |

**The Gap:** No affordable, instant, AI-powered valuation tool specifically for first-time buyers and SMB sellers.
